### What did not get promoted as a general corruption pattern

I tested whether this could be generalized right now as a broad national
pattern for higher-education capture. The current graph does show:

- multiple higher-ed institutions with SECOP alias bridges
- multiple higher-ed institutions with public procurement links
- a few institutions with internal surname-based family ties

But those signals do not combine cleanly enough across the national graph to
justify a new promoted pattern beyond the existing `education_control_capture`
case framing. On the current data, San Jose remains the strongest defended
investigation because it has:

- official MEN control nodes
- a direct SECOP alias bridge
- real interadministrative agreements
- additional named actors in institutional documents
- a public RUT that turns several reported names into documentary nodes
- a verifiable commercial-registry layer around Andres Mendez and Mauricio Guevara
- public portfolio / marketing traces around BLUHARTMANN and HazteProfesional
